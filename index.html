Dog History and Fun Facts

Dogs have been human companions for thousands of years. They are known for their loyalty and playful nature. Did you know that dogs are descendants of wolves? Over generations, humans have selectively bred dogs for various traits, leading to the diverse breeds we have today.

Here are some fun facts about dogs:

  • Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, which is 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans.
  • There are over 340 different dog breeds worldwide, each with its unique characteristics.
  • Dogs are known to be excellent swimmers, with certain breeds naturally inclined to water activities.
